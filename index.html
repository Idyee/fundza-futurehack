The Problem in Education

The major problem in learning is that it is 2D. Most students are visual leaners and imagination is not common to everyone and not being able to imagine means that you cannot learn effectively.

Our Solution

Our solution to the problem is to provide learning materials in 3D. The material will be available for a desktop and mobile browser, and Google Cardbox and other equivalent VR headsets.

Immersive Experience

The user will be able to experience learning in virtual reality as if he was there. They will be able to interact with the environment.
